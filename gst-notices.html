Practical steps

  1. Prepare reconciliation between GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B for the period cited.
  2. Collect invoice copies and payment challans where applicable.
  3. Draft a concise reply attaching reconciliation and annexures.
  4. Pay admitted tax/interest with challan and submit proof if liability exists.
