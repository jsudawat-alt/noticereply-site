Section 245 — Adjustment of Refund

Section 245 notices relate to adjustment of refund against outstanding demand or dues of the taxpayer. They require careful reconciliation of ledgers and assessment orders to confirm correctness.

Key checks

  • Confirm whether the refund has been correctly credited and whether any demand exists to justify adjustment.
  • Check the assessment years and references where adjustment is claimed.
  • Verify ledger (TDS, advance tax, self-assessment payments) for matching entries.

Reply steps

  1. Prepare a ledger reconciliation showing refund credit and demands across assessment years.
  2. If the adjustment is incorrect, prepare a short factual reply with supporting documents and payment proofs.
  3. If the adjustment is correct but requires clarification, request a statement of account and provide your reconciled statement.
  4. Keep proof of any payment or communication and confirm follow-up timelines in the reply.

Sample reply (editable)

To
The Assessing Officer,
[Address]

Subject: Reply to notice under Section 245 dated [dd-mm-yyyy] – PAN [●]

Respected Sir/Madam,

1. We refer to the notice dated [date] which indicates adjustment of refund for AY [●] against demand for AY [●].

2. Enclosed is a ledger reconciliation (Annexure A) showing refund credited on [date] and the demands outstanding. We request the AO to kindly provide the basis of adjustment (orders / references) if different from our records.

3. If any genuine demand exists, we shall arrange reconciliation/ payment. Otherwise, we request restoration of refund and correction of the ledger.

Yours faithfully,
[Name / Authorised Signatory]
