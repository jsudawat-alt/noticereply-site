About NOTICEREPLY

I am a practicing Chartered Accountant with over 25 years of experience in Income Tax, GST, and business compliance advisory. Having represented clients in departmental assessments, audits, and appellate proceedings, I realized one consistent problem — most taxpayers and even professionals struggle to draft effective replies to notices.

That’s how NOTICEREPLY was born — to simplify the process of understanding, analyzing, and replying to Income Tax and GST notices with legal precision and practical clarity.

Professional Background

  • Fellow Chartered Accountant (FCA) with over 25 years of practice in Direct and Indirect taxation.
  • Alumni of Indian Institute of Management (IIM) - Ahmedabad.

Vision

To build India’s most trusted online resource for notice handling — where both taxpayers and professionals can find authentic guidance, templates, and clarity rooted in law and experience.

Our Philosophy

  • Accuracy: Every guide and template is legally valid and professionally vetted.
  • Clarity: Complex legal sections explained in plain language.
  • Practicality: Real-world drafting experience built into every format.
  • Continuity: Regular updates from notifications, circulars, and court rulings.

Expert Areas

  • Income Tax Assessments & Reassessments
  • GST Scrutiny, Audit & Adjudication
  • Refund & Demand Disputes
  • Search, Seizure & Appeal Matters
  • Legal Drafting of Replies, Submissions, and Appeals

“In tax practice, a timely and well-drafted reply is your best defense.”

At NOTICEREPLY, we stand by that principle — helping you respond smartly, professionally, and confidently.
