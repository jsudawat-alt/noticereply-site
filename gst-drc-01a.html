DRC-01A is a notice commonly issued to taxpayers for under-reported liability or mismatch in returns. This guide explains what the notice seeks, how to reconcile, and a draft reply you can adapt.

What it is

DRC-01A typically alleges short payment of tax or mismatch between returns (GSTR-1 vs GSTR-3B). The taxpayer must reconcile sales, outward supplies, and tax payments and submit supporting invoices or explanations.

Checklist

  • GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B for the period
  • Sales register and invoices
  • Payment challans and ITC reconciliations
  • Any corrective returns filed

Practical steps

  1. Prepare a reconciliation table showing reported vs actual figures.
  2. If mismatch is clerical, file corrections and attach reconciliation.
  3. If tax is due, arrange payment and provide challan details.
  4. Submit a concise reply with annexures to the officer within time limits.
