ASMT-10 — Assessment / Discrepancy Notice

ASMT-10 is issued where the assessing officer finds discrepancies during assessment proceedings. The notice invites explanation and documents — it may lead to reassessment or demand if unexplained.

Checklist

  • GSTR-1, GSTR-3B and reconciliations for the relevant months
  • Invoices and supporting delivery / acceptance proofs
  • Payment receipts, bank traces and E-way bill records

How to respond

  1. Map each discrepancy point to documents and produce a reconciled table.
  2. Provide explanations—timeline of transactions, corrections made, and any subsequent amendments filed.
  3. Where mistakes are clerical, explain and provide corrective filing references; where disputes exist, attach legal note and supporting judgments.

Sample reply (editable)

To
The Proper Officer,
[GST Office Address]

Subject: Reply to ASMT-10 dated [dd-mm-yyyy] — GSTIN [●]

Respected Sir/Madam,

1. We refer to ASMT-10 dated [date] indicating discrepancies for the period [mm/yyyy]. Enclosed are reconciliations and supporting documents:

   - Annexure A: GSTR-1 vs GSTR-3B reconciliation.
   - Annexure B: Invoice copies and delivery acknowledgements.
   - Annexure C: Payment challans and E-way bills.

2. The discrepancies arise primarily due to [reason — late upload / system lag / credit notes]. Copies of corrective returns (if filed) are attached.

3. We request the officer to consider the annexures and provide guidance on any further steps.

Yours faithfully,
[Authorised Signatory]
