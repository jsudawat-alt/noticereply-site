DRC-01 — Demand Creation Notice

DRC-01 is the official notice of demand issued after investigation or reconciliation. It sets out the amount of tax, interest and penalty determined to be due and provides instructions for payment or contestation.

What to confirm immediately

  • Exact demand amount and the period it covers.
  • Whether any credit or payment has already been accounted for.
  • Deadlines for payment and the consequences of non-payment (recovery).

Checklist

  • Reconciliation showing department vs your books
  • Proof of payments, challans and credits
  • Any earlier communications or corrective filings

Reply strategy

  1. Provide a concise computation showing basis of your figures and attach supporting documents.
  2. If demand is admitted, pay immediately and provide challan evidence in reply.
  3. If disputed, file appeal/rectification and furnish grounds along with supporting documents and request stay of recovery if eligible.

Sample reply (editable)

To
The Proper Officer,
[GST Office Address]

Subject: Reply to DRC-01 dated [dd-mm-yyyy] — GSTIN [●]

Respected Sir/Madam,

1. We acknowledge the receipt of DRC-01 dated [date] showing demand of Rs. [●] for the period [mm/yyyy]. We enclose our reconciliation (Annexure A) and supporting documents.

2. [If payment made] We have paid Rs. [●] on [date] challan no. [●] (copy enclosed). Kindly update the ledger.

3. [If disputed] We dispute the demand for reasons detailed in Annexure B and request reconsideration. We have initiated appeal / rectification on [date] (copy enclosed).

Yours faithfully,
[Authorised Signatory]
