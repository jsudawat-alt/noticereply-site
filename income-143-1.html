Section 143(1) intimations are automated computations or adjustments performed by the Income Tax Department. This page explains common triggers, documents to check, and a ready-to-edit reply draft you can use.

Summary

143(1) intimations are generally for arithmetic corrections, incorrect or missing TDS reporting, mismatch in gross receipts, or computational changes. First step is to compare the department‚Äôs computation with your filed return and your records.

Checklist (documents to gather)

  • Filed return (XML / PDF)
  • Bank statements for the relevant year
  • TDS certificates (Form 16 / 16A / 26AS)
  • Sales / purchase registers or reconciliation schedules
  • Any supporting invoices / payment receipts

Practical approach

  1. Reconcile the department‚Äôs figures vs your books.
  2. If it‚Äôs a genuine mismatch, prepare reconciliation table and attach supporting docs.
  3. If the intimation is incorrect, prepare a short factual reply with annexures.
