Section 142(1) — Inquiry before Assessment

A notice under Section 142(1) is issued to seek information and documents before completing an assessment. It outlines specific details the Assessing Officer requires to verify the return.

Common reasons for 142(1)

  • Clarify discrepancies in declared income or expenses.
  • Request books, invoices, bank statements or other records.
  • Seek explanations on transactions that appear unusual.

Checklist (documents to gather)

  • Books of accounts, ledgers, and trial balance
  • Bank statements and payment receipts
  • Supplier/customer invoices and contracts
  • Previous assessment orders / correspondence

Step-by-step reply approach

  1. Carefully read the notice and itemise each document or explanation requested.
  2. Gather supporting documents, index them (Annexure A, B…), and prepare a concise covering reply.
  3. If more time is needed, request an extension with a reasoned explanation before the due date.
  4. File the reply through the e-filling portal or send physical documents as instructed and retain proof of submission.

Practical tips

  • Provide reconciliations (books vs return) for the specific items raised.
  • Organise annexures in the same order as listed in the reply for easy verification.
  • Address each query succinctly—don’t add unrelated information that could trigger further questions.

Sample reply (editable)

To
The Assessing Officer,
[Address]

Subject: Reply to notice under Section 142(1) dated [dd-mm-yyyy] – PAN [●], AY [●]

Respected Sir/Madam,

1. We refer to the notice dated [date] seeking documents and information in respect of PAN [●] for AY [●].

2. In response to the queries, we enclose the following:
   - Annexure A: Books of accounts and trial balance for FY [●].
   - Annexure B: Bank statements (Jan–Dec [year]).
   - Annexure C: Invoice copies and reconciliations.

3. For convenience, we have prepared a short note explaining the reconciliation (Annexure D) and remain available for any further clarification.

Yours faithfully,
[Name / Authorised Signatory]
